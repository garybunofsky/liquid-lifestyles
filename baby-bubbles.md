A calm, positive first relationship with the water
Baby Bubbles® is designed to help infants and toddlers feel comfortable, curious, and secure in the water—at their own pace.

These classes focus on early water awareness, trust, and positive experiences that set the foundation for confidence later on.

This is not about “teaching babies to swim.”

It’s about helping them feel safe, supported, and happy in and around the water.

What parents typically notice:
Babies who remain calm and relaxed in the water

Growing comfort with gentle movement, floating, and submersion

Increased curiosity and engagement instead of fear or resistance

Smiles, laughter, and positive anticipation of swim time

Thoughtfully structured for babies and parents

THE BABY BUBBLES® APPROACH
Building comfort, trust, and confidence before independence
Baby Bubbles® is intentionally designed around one simple belief:

A child’s relationship with the water matters more than early performance.

Before skills, before strokes, before independence, babies need to feel calm, secure, and supported in the water. When that foundation is strong, progress happens naturally—without pressure, fear, or resistance.

Why Baby Bubbles® looks different
Many infant swim programs focus on doing:

floating, kicking, submerging, holding breath.

Baby Bubbles® focuses first on being:

feeling safe

staying calm

trusting the environment

enjoying the experience

We believe early exposure should build positive associations, not compliance. Babies who feel rushed or overwhelmed may appear to “perform” in the moment—but often struggle later when expectations increase.

Comfort comes first
In Baby Bubbles®, comfort is not a milestone to check off.

It is the environment we protect.

Comfort allows babies to:

regulate their breathing

remain relaxed during movement

explore without panic

recover quickly from surprises

This is why our classes emphasize:

gentle pacing

predictable routines

responsive coaching

a calm sensory environment

Trust is the real curriculum
Babies don’t learn by instruction.

They learn by experience and relationship.

Baby Bubbles® is built around trust:

trust in the water

trust in the caregiver

trust in the coach

trust in the environment

Every activity is designed to strengthen that trust—not test it.

Why parent participation matters
A parent or caregiver is always in the water during Baby Bubbles®.

This isn’t just for safety—it’s for emotional security.

When babies are supported by someone they trust, they are more willing to:

try new sensations

tolerate brief discomfort

remain calm during transitions

build confidence that transfers beyond the pool

Our coaches guide the experience, while parents provide reassurance and connection.

Developmentally responsive structure
Baby Bubbles® serves children 6–30 months old, but no two babies develop on the same timeline.

Instead of separating rigid age groups, we:

dynamically group babies by age and comfort level

adjust activities in real time

meet each child where they are that day

This flexibility allows us to respect:

physical development

emotional readiness

sensory tolerance

individual temperament

Calm by design
Baby Bubbles® classes are intentionally structured to reduce stimulation and distraction.

Key elements include:

warm water (≈90°)

exclusive use of the pool during class

multiple coaches in the water

limited class size

This creates a focused, low-pressure environment where babies and parents can remain present, relaxed, and engaged.

What Baby Bubbles® is not
Baby Bubbles® is not:

survival swimming

performance-based instruction

rushed skill acquisition

a test of bravery or compliance

Those approaches may show quick results—but often undermine long-term confidence.

How Baby Bubbles® supports long-term success
Babies who experience calm, positive water interactions early are more likely to:

transition smoothly into independent lessons

tolerate submersion without fear

remain relaxed during skill development

build confidence that holds up outside the pool

Baby Bubbles® is the beginning of that pathway—not a shortcut.

A foundation, not a finish line
Baby Bubbles® prepares children for what comes next by ensuring their first experiences in the water are:

positive

supportive

pressure-free

confidence-building

When babies are ready, independence follows naturally.
