Three Levels

Which level fits your child?

Level 01

Liquid Bubbles 1

Ages 2.5 – 10 (grouped by age)

A calm, comfortable first experience in the water

Best for children new to swimming or those who need a thoughtful, pressure-free start. Children learn to trust the water and the coach — fear gives way to curiosity, hesitation melts, and confidence begins to emerge.

Parents typically notice

  • Less anxiety and resistance around the pool
  • Willingness to put their face in the water at their own pace
  • Growing enjoyment floating, going underwater, and moving through the water
  • Smiles replacing stress as kids start to enjoy swim time

LB-1 is best if your child

  • Is new to swim lessons
  • Feels unsure or cautious around water
  • Is not yet able to float independently
  • Is not yet able to go underwater and return to the surface

Level 02

Liquid Bubbles 2

Ages 2.5 – 10 (grouped by age)

Advancing independence and control in the water

For children comfortable being in and under the water and ready to move with more independence. Confidence becomes capability as kids learn to work with the water — moving, balancing, and breathing intentionally.

Parents typically notice

  • A craving for independence and control in the water
  • Swimming short distances without assistance
  • Improved breath control and composure underwater
  • Confidently navigating the pool on their own

LB-2 is best when your child

  • Is comfortable being in and under the water
  • Can float independently on their front and back
  • Can retrieve objects underwater and return to the surface
  • Is beginning to swim short distances independently

Level 03

Liquid Bubbles 3

Ages 5 – 10

Strengthening swimming that holds up in real life

For children who already swim independently and are ready to refine control, endurance, and composure. Confidence and competence become consistency — kids swim with purpose, manage breathing, and stay balanced across longer distances.

Parents typically notice

  • Calm, controlled, deliberate swimming movements
  • Sustainable breathing without panic or fatigue
  • Improved balance, alignment, and efficiency in the water
  • Greater confidence handling deeper water and longer swims

LB-3 is best when your child

  • Swims independently across the pool without stopping
  • Can retrieve objects from depth and return to the surface calmly
  • Maintains breathing control while swimming longer distances
  • Is comfortable treading water for short periods without flotation
