At Liquid Lifestyles, we believe in the power of personalized instruction to unlock every swimmer’s potential. Our private lessons are designed to meet the unique needs of each individual. Whether you’re looking to refine your technique, improve fitness, or prioritize safety, our dedicated coaches are here to support you every step of the way. With a focus on hands-on instruction and a proven curriculum, our experienced coaches are committed to helping swimmers of all ages and abilities drastically improve their skills.

We understand that no two swimmers are alike, which is why we do not employ a one-size-fits-all curriculum. Instead, our coaches adapt their teaching methods to suit your needs, providing targeted feedback and skill-based drills to practice between lessons. This personalized approach allows you to build confidence, competence, and a lifelong love of swimming.

Each private lesson is 30 minutes in length, recurring weekly on the same day and time. Progression is determined by each individual’s pace, comfort, and goals, ensuring that you continue to find success and confidence with every lesson.

Share your journey with a friend or family member! A second swimmer can be added to any private lesson plan by completing our CUSTOM GROUP LESSON request. Single visit passes are also available by contacting our Customer Care Team and must be booked into the next available time slot.

Help us help you! Before you get started with private lessons please send an “About You” email to our Customer Care Team. By sharing your specific lesson goals, we can tailor your sessions to meet your unique needs and ensure you get the most out of your time with us!
