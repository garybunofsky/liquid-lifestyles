Why families pick our Westlake pool

Built for west-side families who don't have time to waste.

90°F water

Warm enough for the youngest swimmers

No shivering, no fighting to get back in. Our pool is kept at 90°F so even the most reluctant first-timer relaxes faster — and stays in the water longer.

Small classes

Real attention from real instructors

Every group lesson at Westlake is kept small — so your kid actually swims, not waits. All instructors are certified Water Safety Instructors and members of the U.S. Swim School Association.

Year-round indoor

No off-season, no skill loss

Kids who only swim in the summer lose progress every fall. Westlake is open year-round, so what they learn in October sticks through February.

Westlake FAQ

Common questions from west-side families.

  • Where is the Westlake location?

    Westlake, Ohio 44145 — just off I-90, easy access from Bay Village, Rocky River, Avon, and Lakewood. Get directions.

  • What ages do you teach at Westlake?

    From 6 months old through adult. Baby Bubbles (6 mo – 3 yrs) up through Liquid Medals (4 – 12), private lessons at any age, and adult programs for 15 and up — all under one roof.

  • How warm is the pool?

    90°F year-round. Warm enough that even hesitant first-timers stop shivering and start swimming.

  • Do you offer makeup classes?

    Yes — members can book make-ups up to 7 days in advance through the Liquid Lifestyles app or the registration portal, subject to availability.

  • How do I sign up?

    Start with our 2-minute class-finder quiz to land on the right program, then register through the portal. Questions? Call 440-517-SWIM.
