Why Private Lessons?

No two swimmers are alike — so we don't teach with a one-size-fits-all curriculum.

Our coaches adapt their teaching to your goals, with hands-on instruction, targeted feedback, and skill-based drills you can practice between lessons. Whether you want to refine technique, improve fitness, or focus on safety — your coach builds the path that fits.

This personal approach builds confidence, competence, and a lifelong love of swimming.

01 · Length

30 Minutes Weekly

Each lesson is 30 minutes, recurring weekly on the same day and time.

Your Speed
