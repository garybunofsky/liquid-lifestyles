Square One
This level is for adults who are anxious or afraid in and around water and who are uncomfortable going under water or in deep water.

Over the 5-week course students will learn how to become comfortable in water and to move smoothly and independently across short distances.

Through guided discovery exercises and hands-on instruction, our coaches will introduce relaxed breathing techniques and basic balance positions so you can experience weightlessness and relaxation in AND under the surface. 

Classes are taught in the shallow area of the pool and the instructor remains in the water with you at all times.

 PREREQUISITES:

Fear or anxiety around water and/or uncomfortable going underwater

No experience floating or moving through the water independently

Uncomfortable in deep water

GOAL: COMFORT

Guided discovery drills to examine & overcome fears

Relaxed breathing techniques in and under the water

Effortless front and back floats

Introduction to basic self-propulsion techniques

Independent, short-distance swims at and below the surface


Beginner Basics
This class will help you gain confidence in deeper water! Through step-by-step drills you will learn breathing, balance and propulsive techniques that can be utilized in ANY depth of water and will open the doors to a lifetime of possibilities!

Students will learn safe and sustainable deep water treading techniques and whole-body safety strokes that will have you swimming with confidence. 

These lessons are taught in the deep pool and the coach will be in the water with you to support and encourage you every step of the way! 

PREREQUISITES:

Comfortable in and under the water

Able to swim short distances (either head up or head underwater)

Uncomfortable in deep water / unable to tread water

GOAL: INDEPENDENCE

Increase breath control and comfort in any depth of water

Develop deep water self-rescue and water safety techniques

Refinement of basic balancing positions and self-propulsion techniques

Introduction to sustainable whole-body movements and independent breathing sequences

Elementary backstroke


Four Stroke Fundamentals
Swim for fitness, for fun, for life! Gain confidence and increase your overall satisfaction in the water with a broad spectrum of new swim progressions. 

Using a variety of training techniques we will guide you through the basic drill progressions and stroke mechanics of Freestyle, Backstroke, Elementary Backstroke and Breaststroke. Combined, these skills will offer new and exciting challenges and be a welcomed addition in your workout design. 

These classes are taught in the deep pool and the coach will remain on deck.

PREREQUISITES:

Comfortable underwater and in deep water

Can sustainably tread water (eyes and ears above)

Can independently swim 25+ yards (any stroke/style)

Demonstrates comfort on the back

GOAL: ENDURANCE

Learn basic short-axis and long-axis drill progressions; Freestyle, Backstroke, Elementary Backstroke, Breaststroke

Heightened body awareness and self-critique

Improved balance, symmetry and range of motion across all muscular systems


Next Level Freestyle
For fitness swimmers or triathletes serious about improving their freestyle stroke mechanics and increasing their overall efficiency, control, and speed in the water.

This 5-week course will progress using our proven drill progression that focuses on the fundamental swimming principles of balance, length, rotation and timing.

These lessons are taught in the deep pool and the coach will remain on deck. 

PREREQUISITES:

Can comfortably swim 100+ yards continuously (freestyle)

Can swim underwater

Comfortable in deep water

Interested in improving FREESTYLE efficiency and speed

GOAL: IMPROVED FREESTYLE

Develop proper Freestyle techniques 

Increase efficiency and speed 

Translate rotational power into forward motion.

Integrate bilateral breathing techniques and timing

Heightened body awareness and self-critique
