Step up to the block with the skills, speed, and strategy of the world's best.

Small-group classes (2:1 ratio) for competitive swimmers ages 6–12. Three Levels

Bronze. Silver. Gold.

Level 01

Liquid Bronze

Ages 6 – 12

Build the competitive basics

For swimmers who already have basic stroke proficiency and breath control. Refine timing, learn the four competitive strokes, and start training with real metrics.

Primary focus

  • Refine long-axis stroke timing (Freestyle & Backstroke)
  • Introduce Breaststroke timing and Butterfly basics
  • Develop competitive starts, flip-turn basics, and finishes
  • Build endurance & intro to training metrics (DPS, Tempo, Time, RI)
  • Improve breath management and underwater swimming

Prerequisites

Pass the Liquid Bubbles 5-Star Safety Test in under 15 minutes:

  • Comfortably swim 25 yards full-stroke freestyle & backstroke
  • 25 yards each of Freestyle, Backstroke & Breaststroke kick (legal)
  • Swim below water (3+ ft) using surface dives and double arm pulls
  • Wall jump → 1 min treading → surface dive to object (6+ ft, no goggles)

Level 02

Liquid Silver

Ages 7 – 12

Stroke refinement & rotation

For swimmers already legal in freestyle, backstroke, and breaststroke who want to refine technique and learn butterfly.

Primary focus

  • Refine long-axis stroke timing and positioning
  • Develop full-body Butterfly with whole-body undulations
  • Improve starts, turns, and finishes for all 4 strokes
  • Reinforce USA Swimming rules & competition regulations
  • Extend underwater swimming and breath management

Prerequisites

  • 1 full season of swim team experience
  • Swim 75 yards continuously with legal Freestyle, Backstroke, Breaststroke
  • Streamline dive from a wall or starting block
  • 2 min deep-water tread → surface dive to object (6+ ft, no goggles)

Level 03

Liquid Gold

Ages 8 – 12

Bust bad habits, build elite form

For competitive swimmers ready to train like the world's best — focused drills, intelligent training sets, and impeccable form.

Class focus

  • Advanced technique work in all 4 competitive strokes
  • Finishing touches on starts, turns, finishes, and IM transitions
  • Training metrics: DPS, Tempo, Time
  • Integrated training tools to lock in proper mechanics
  • Race strategy & goal setting

Prerequisites

  • Currently on a competitive team or 1+ full season experience
  • Legal in all 4 competitive strokes
