One of the only swim schools in Cleveland that specializes in adult lessons.

Whether you've never swum a stroke, you're nervous around water, or you want to perfect your freestyle — we have a starting point for you. Find your level below. Custom clinics are created when there's enough interest.

Four Levels

Beginner to elite. Find your fit.

Level 01

Square One

Goal: Comfort

Conquer fear and find your float

For adults who feel anxious or afraid in and around water. Over 5 weeks, you'll learn to feel comfortable in the water and move smoothly across short distances.

Classes are taught in the shallow end — your coach is in the water with you the whole time.

What you'll learn

  • Guided drills to examine and overcome fear
  • Relaxed breathing in and under the water
  • Effortless front and back floats
  • Basic self-propulsion techniques
  • Independent short-distance swims

Best when you

  • Feel anxious in or around water
  • Are uncomfortable going underwater
  • Have no experience floating or swimming independently

Level 02

Beginner Basics

Goal: Independence

Get confident in deep water

Step-by-step drills for breathing, balance, and propulsion that work in any depth. Learn safe deep-water treading and whole-body safety strokes.

Taught in the deep pool. Your coach stays in the water to support you.

What you'll learn

  • Breath control and comfort in any depth
  • Deep-water self-rescue and safety techniques
  • Refined balance and self-propulsion
  • Sustainable whole-body movement & breathing
  • Elementary backstroke

Best when you

  • Are comfortable in and under the water
  • Can swim short distances (head up or down)
  • Are not yet comfortable in deep water or treading

Level 03

Four-Stroke Fundamentals

Goal: Endurance

Swim for fitness, fun, life

Open up a broader spectrum of swimming. Learn drill progressions and stroke mechanics for Freestyle, Backstroke, Elementary Backstroke, and Breaststroke.

Taught in the deep pool. Coach on deck.

What you'll learn

  • Short- and long-axis drill progressions
  • Freestyle, Backstroke, Elementary Back, Breaststroke fundamentals
  • Heightened body awareness and self-critique
  • Improved balance, symmetry, and range of motion

Best when you

  • Are comfortable underwater and in deep water
  • Can sustainably tread water (eyes & ears above)
  • Can swim 25+ yards independently (any stroke)
  • Are comfortable on your back

Level 04

Next-Level Freestyle

Goal: Improved Freestyle

For fitness swimmers & triathletes

Get serious about freestyle mechanics, efficiency, and speed. Our 5-week course progresses through balance, length, rotation, and timing.

Taught in the deep pool. Coach on deck.

What you'll learn

  • Proper freestyle techniques
  • Efficiency, speed, and rotational power
  • Bilateral breathing techniques and timing
  • Heightened body awareness and self-critique

Best when you

  • Can swim 100+ yards continuously freestyle
  • Can swim underwater and feel confident in deep water
  • Want to improve freestyle efficiency & speed

No Judgment. Just Progress.
