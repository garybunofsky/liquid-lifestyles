
Liquid Bubbles — A Path That Works
Our Learn To Swim Programs for Kids Ages 2.5–10

Liquid Bubbles® is our proven learn-to-swim program designed to help young swimmers build comfort, confidence, and real swimming competence. We start by helping children feel comfortable in the water — because comfort builds confidence, confidence leads to sustainable swimming skills, and skills create lasting safety. Families tell us again and again:

“We see confidence grow every week — fewer tears, more smiles, and progress that really sticks.”

WHICH LEVEL IS best for your child?

Liquid Bubbles 1
Ages: 2.5 - 10 years old (grouped by age*)

A calm, comfortable first experience in the water

Best for children who are new to swimming or need a thoughtful, pressure-free start.

Liquid Bubbles® 1 is designed to help children feel comfortable in the water from day one. Children learn to trust the water and the coach— fear gives way to curiosity, hesitation melts and confidence begins to emerge.

Parents typically notice:

Less anxiety and resistance around the pool

Willingness to put their face in the water at their own pace

Growing enjoyment floating, going underwater, and moving through the water

Smiles replacing stress as children begin to enjoy swim time

We don’t rush children through skills in LB1. Progress happens when a child is ready, not after a predefined number of lessons. Confidence and repetition is what makes learning new skills really stick.

LB-1 is BEST if your child:
Is new to swim lessons

Feels unsure or cautious around water

Is not yet able to float independently

Is not yet able to go under water and return to the surface independently.

*Classes are grouped by age: 2.5-4 yrs. and  5-10 yrs.


Liquid Bubbles 2
Ages: 2.5–10 years old (grouped by age*)

Advancing independence and control in the water.

Best for children who are comfortable being in and under the water and are ready to move with more independence.

Liquid Bubbles® 2 is designed for children who are ready to begin controlling their movements and breathing. In this level, confidence turns into capability as children learn to work with, instead of against, the water to move, balance, and breathe intentionally.

Parents typically notice:

Craving  independence and control in the water

Swimming short distances without assistance

Improved breath control and composure underwater

Confidently navigating the pool on their own

Children in LB-2 begin to experience what it feels like to move intentionally in the water, not just participate in activities. Skills are built progressively so confidence remains intact as independence increases.

LB-2 IS BEST WHEN YOUR CHILD:
Is comfortable being in and under the water

Can float independently on their front and back

Can retrieve objects underwater and return to the surface

Is beginning to swim short distances independently

*Classes are grouped by age: 2.5–4 yrs. and 5–10 yrs.


Liquid Bubbles 3
Ages: 5–10 years old

Strengthening consistent and competent swimming that holds up in real life

Best for children who already swim independently and are ready to refine sustainable, composed swimming skills.

Liquid Bubbles® 3 is designed for children who are comfortable moving independently in the water and are ready to refine control, endurance, and composure. In this level, confidence and competence becomes consistency as children learn to swim with purpose, manage breathing over distance, and stay balanced and effective in a variety of situations.

Parents typically notice:

Calm, controlled, deliberate swimming movements

Sustainable breathing without panic or fatigue

Improved balance, alignment, and efficiency in the water

Greater confidence handling deeper water and longer swims

Children in LB-3 are no longer just “able to swim.” They learn to swim with control and confidence that lasts, setting the stage for lifelong enjoyment and real-world water safety.

LB-3 IS BEST WHEN YOUR CHILD:
Swims independently across the pool without stopping

Can retrieve objects from depth and return to the surface calmly

Maintains breathing control while swimming longer distances

Is comfortable treading water for short periods without flotation
